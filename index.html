SDE ensemble: log₁₀ Var[θ₁](t) of the double pendulum under a stochastic torque

          

        

        

          

            
Inverse · Surrogate · Stochastic

          

          

            ∿Stochastic ensemble (SDE)›
            

              

                Langevin ensemble of the double pendulum with additive angular-velocity noise (a stochastic
                torque), advanced with Euler–Maruyama and accumulated with Welford online moments.
              

              
              
            

          

          

            ⊶Inverse problem (parameter fit)›
            

              

                Recover g from a synthetic (θ₁, θ₂) trajectory by nonlinear least squares
                (Levenberg–Marquardt) with the engine's own RHS in the fit loop, reporting the standard error.
              

              
              
            

          

          

            ∑PCE surrogate (Sobol)›
            

              

                Polynomial-chaos surrogate on a total-degree Legendre basis (independent uniform inputs)
                gives an analytic Sobol variance decomposition of a test response.
