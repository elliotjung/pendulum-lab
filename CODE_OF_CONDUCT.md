# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free project for contributors of
every age, background, identity, and level of experience. Be respectful,
assume good intent, give actionable technical feedback, cite evidence, and
avoid personal attacks, discriminatory language, intimidation, or publishing
someone else's private information.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and comments that violate these
standards, and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when
necessary. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented, and
focused on protecting the community.

Report conduct concerns privately to `jung.elliot@gmail.com`. Reports will be
handled confidentially; do not use public issues for sensitive details.

This policy is based on the Contributor Covenant 2.1 and applies in project
spaces and when representing the project in public.
